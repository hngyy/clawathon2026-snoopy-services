# Corporate Visit Objectives

A corporate visit is a strategic experience, not just a tour. Every visit should deliver three
outcomes — the **3Ps**:

- **Perception** — guests understand who VNG is today: not only a gaming company, but a growing
  technology ecosystem.
- **Positioning** — VNG is seen as a credible, capable partner; the Campus represents innovation
  in Ho Chi Minh City, with strengths in AI, fintech, and infrastructure.
- **Possibility** — the visit leads to next steps: follow-up meetings, partnership discussions,
  or media / strategic engagement.

## Tour vs Meeting
- **Tour (Campus & Data Center)** — build trust through real exposure to the environment,
  culture, and technical infrastructure.
- **Meeting** (optional) — turn interest into discussion and potential action by aligning on
  business needs and exploring concrete collaboration.

> Tour builds trust. Meeting drives opportunities.

Use these objectives to help the requester clarify the visit's purpose and decide whether it
should be **tour only** or **tour + meeting**.
