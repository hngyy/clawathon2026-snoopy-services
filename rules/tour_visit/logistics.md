# Corporate Visit Logistics

A visit runs across three phases, all coordinated by the Corporate Communications (CC) team.

## Before — Preparation & alignment
**Intake & mapping:** understand the guest profile and background, identify the purpose, define
the priority tier, map relevant Business Units, and propose the structure (tour only / tour +
meeting).

**Logistics checklist:**
- Schedule & rooms — schedule alignment and meeting-room booking
- Tea break & refreshments — tea break, water, refreshments for guests
- Welcome setup — welcome screen and guest greeting
- Partner gifts — gifts prepared for visiting partners
- Technical setup — laptop, pointer, presentation materials
- Media & recording — photo / video support and audio recording if required

**Speakers (for meetings):** select by relevant expertise and appropriate seniority; align on
key messages, presentation flow, talking points, and expected questions.

## During — Execution & experience delivery
1. Reception — welcome guests at the lobby; brief the agenda and flow
2. Tour (core experience) — Campus and/or Data Center tour; share culture, business, technology
3. Meeting (if applicable) — presentations and discussions; manage agenda and timing

CC coordinates the flow, ensures message consistency, and handles on-site issues.

## After — Follow-up & content leverage
- Content processing — transcribe key discussions if needed; select and organize photos
- Internal leverage — share materials with PR and EB; amplify via media and internal channels
- External follow-up — send visit recap and retouched photos; provide materials; collect feedback
