# Supporting Teams

The coordinator (owner) loops in these teams during the Before phase to prepare a visit. Use
this to decide which team to notify for a given preparation task (see the notify_team tool).

## Internal teams
- **BIE — Brand & Internal Events**
  - Photography *(Trello)*
  - Welcome screen design *(Trello)*
  - Partner gifts & tea break *(Email)*
- **EB — Employer Branding** *(Email)*
  - Welcome screen display & presentation
  - Social & internal communications
- **PR — Public Relations** *(Email)*
  - Media & press coverage

## External / cross-functional teams
- **IT** *(Email — request.it@vng.com.vn)*
  - Equipment & technical setup (laptop, screen, projector, system support)
- **AF — Administration & Facilities** *(Email — afservices@vng.com.vn)*
  - Room booking & setup
  - Water & refreshments

> Channel shows how each team is reached: **Trello** for visual/production tasks, **Email** for
> the rest. (Trello card creation is being wired; for now all notifications are sent by email.)
