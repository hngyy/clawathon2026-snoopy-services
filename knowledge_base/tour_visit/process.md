# Corporate Visit Process

## What it is
A VNG **Corporate Visit** is a strategic touchpoint for external guests — partners, clients,
students, and stakeholders. It consists of a **Tour** (Campus and/or Data Center) and an
**optional Meeting**. It is organized by an internal **requester** and coordinated by the
**Corporate Communications (CC) team** (the visit **owner**).

## Who does what
- **Requester** (internal VNG employee): initiates the request and provides guest details.
- **Owner / CC coordinator**: reviews, maps Business Units, defines the structure (tour only or
  tour + meeting), and loops in supporting teams to prepare.
- **Supporting teams** (BIE, EB, PR, IT, AF): handle logistics, media, technical setup, and
  facilities. See get_supporting_teams.

## Request flow
1. The requester submits a visit request with all required details.
2. The coordinator is notified by email and reviews the request.
3. The coordinator coordinates with the relevant supporting teams to prepare (Before phase).
4. The requester receives an email confirmation with the agreed date and instructions.

## What the requester must provide
- Requester name (internal organizer)
- Guest organization / company
- Guest profile / background (who the guests are, seniority)
- Visit type: **tour only** or **tour + meeting**
- Preferred visit date
- Number of guests
- Purpose of the visit
- Contact email
- Meeting topic — only if the visit includes a meeting
- Partner gift — optional

For booking constraints (advance notice, group-size limits, hours, restricted areas), see
get_booking_restrictions. For the goals that shape a good visit, see get_visit_objectives.
